2. Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my past readings?

Navigate to the History tab from the main menu. Here you'll find all your saved readings organized by date. Tap on any reading to view the complete details.

What does it mean when a card appears reversed?

Reversed cards often represent blocked or inverted energies of the card's upright meaning. Our app provides specific interpretations for both upright and reversed positions.

How do I get AI interpretation for my readings?

AI interpretations are available with our premium subscription. After drawing your cards, tap the "AI Reading" button to receive personalized insights based on your specific question and cards.

Can I customize the app appearance?

Yes! Go to Settings and select "Appearance" to change themes, card backs, and other visual elements of the app.

How do I cancel or manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to your iPhone's Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions, find TarotSphere, and manage your subscription options there.

3. Troubleshooting

The app is crashing or freezing

Try these steps in order:

  1. Close the app completely and restart it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Check for app updates in the App Store
  4. Make sure your iOS is updated to the latest version
  5. If problems persist, try uninstalling and reinstalling the app (your reading history is backed up to iCloud if enabled)
My saved readings are missing

If you've enabled iCloud sync in Settings, make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID and have iCloud enabled for TarotSphere. If not using iCloud, readings are stored locally and may be lost if the app is uninstalled.

AI interpretations are unavailable

Check your internet connection, as AI features require an active connection. Also verify that your subscription is active by going to the Subscription page in the app.
